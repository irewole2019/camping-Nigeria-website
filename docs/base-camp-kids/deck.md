## 6. Block 3 / Rotation 1 — Camp Skills: Tent Pitch Race

**10:45 AM to 11:45 AM**

**Actions**

| Time | Action |
|---|---|
| 10:45 | Houses arrive at activity field. Facilitator demos tent setup (5 min). |

**Owners**

- Safety Lead (spotting pegs, mallets)
- House Leaders (with their group)
- Photographer (action shots)

**Watch Out**

- NO hard pegs anywhere near the under-8s. Rubber mallets only.
- Keep the 4-7 zone and 8-12 zone physically separate.
- Photographer priority: slow-mo reel of the winning tent going up.
